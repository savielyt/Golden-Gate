Hours Of Golden Gates Bridge

Enjoy guided tours and hear fascinating stories about the Golden Gate Bridge at the Round House. Guided 45- and 60-minute walking tours are offered day and evening, spring through fall. Tours bring to life the people behind the bridge, the genius of its engineering, and the myths surrounding its famous span. For tickets, call 877-349-7625.
