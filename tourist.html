Tourist Atrractions Of The Golden Gates Bridge

The Golden Gate Bridge is one of the most breathtakingly beautiful walks and bike rides in the world. Biking across the Golden Gate Bridge is a thrilling experience and the half-hour walk allows you to feel the atmosphere and enjoy amazing views of Downtown San Francisco and the bay. Cyclists have free access to the East and West sidewalks 24 hours a day. Pedestrians can walk on the East sidewalk facing San Francisco from 5 a.m. to 6:30 p.m. Walk or bike to the Golden Gate National Parks with picturesque trails and vista points on either end.
